ABOUT THE PROJECT

“protest postcards” initiative took place between april and june 2025 and was created in response to the global rise of protests and movements advocating for change. the project served as a platform for visual activism, amplifying messages of resistance from artists around the world.

we provided a digital postcard template and invited everyone to decorate it with messages of their choice, using any medium, any style and any theme that resonated with them. what emerged was a collective digital exhibition of protest art that transcends borders and unites voices worldwide. each postcard is it's own voice and story, and a piece of a collective movement.

you can find contact details for each artist linked under their piece - take a moment to explore, connect with brilliant artists and immerse youself in the digital exhibition of global resistance.
